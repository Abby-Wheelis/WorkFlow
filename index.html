in the waiting

I know seasons change

and love remains

but how do I worship

in the waiting.

You are greater

than highs and lows

but how do I remember

in the waiting.

You made the stars

and You call them by name

but how do I trust

in the waiting

I've been changed

by what I've been shown

but how do I see

in the waitng.

But even though

I worship in song

and remember with tears

and hesitate to trust

and struggle to see.

You are here.

In the whisper.

In the waiting.

And so I wait.
